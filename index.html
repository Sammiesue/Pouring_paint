Pouring Acrylic History

Pouring acrylics has become a rather famous style of art where artists pour different colors of thinned out acrylic paint on a canvas to create a marbled effect. With some practice and tips, the result is can be quite beautiful. Plus, the process is extremely fun! This unique form of art began with an artist named David Alfaro Siqueiros. He simply poured different colors on top of the canvas and was fascinated with the his newly made masterpiece. His original work is pictured below.
